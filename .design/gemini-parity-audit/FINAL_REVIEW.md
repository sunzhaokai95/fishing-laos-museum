# Gemini Interaction Parity Final Review

Date: 2026-07-13

## Result

The museum now keeps the verified production content and ten-stop route while restoring the interaction depth that was present in the Gemini prototype. The implementation ports behavior and motion systems rather than importing Gemini's invented copy, records, images, or rules.

## Restored Interaction Systems

- Home: bounded pointer ripples and the existing rotating observation index.
- Prologue: continuous 0-100 observation depth with synchronized surface and underwater layers.
- History: 31-node vertical timeline, era filters, inline expansion, and a keyboard-safe image viewer.
- Fish: full-collection distribution statistics, a 12-specimen DOM and Canvas orbit canopy, reshuffle, hover pause, selection drawer, difficulty filter, and filter reset.
- Tackle: real-record system index, SVG relative-load stage, load slider, and contextual readout without invented material thresholds.
- Techniques: four distinct simulators for bait dispersion, rod angle, float signals, and fish control, linked to all 21 curated techniques.
- Anglers: six identity groups in a master-detail stage, a four-question assessment with result dimensions, and a non-predictive folklore draw with reveal and redraw states.
- Culture: folio reading, language index, and metaphysics specimen cabinet as three different structures. Records without real images render no image frame or placeholder.
- Ethics: jurisdiction-aware confirmation workflow that never issues a verdict, plus a local-only pledge with delayed stamp impact.
- Global: route-aware arrow keys, shared Escape behavior, focus restoration, scroll locking, and reduced-motion support.

## Intentional Differences From Gemini

- Current museum names, copy, counts, records, images, and legal-management language remain authoritative.
- No alternate routes, visit-duration choices, scoring, unlocking, visible research sources, or source labels were introduced.
- Missing images stay absent instead of receiving placeholders.
- Regulatory output remains a preparation and manager-lookup workflow, not a legal decision engine.
- Mobile layouts stack dense master-detail stages and retain the single persistent route control.
